[PROJECT_OVERVIEW]

This is a mobile video surveillance system designed specifically for precise and reliable target monitoring. This compact, self-contained kit consists of two modules: a camera positioned near the target, and an operator case featuring a screen that displays a real-time video feed.

Certificate of State Registration of a Computer Program in Russia: №2025668962. The system enables the rapid acquisition of visual information from long distances (up to 3 km) without the need to physically approach the target. It is ideally suited for sports and training applications, as well as for situations where remote visualization of shot impacts is critical.
